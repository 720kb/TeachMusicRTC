You are a Music Teacher

With TeachRTC you can teach music easily to your students by sending them a link to your teaching session.

This app uses WebRTC, the same technology that google hangout uses, it lets you stream video browser-to-browser, directly to your student. The cool thing then, is that has pitch-detection mixed in, so that the student actually gets a music sheet and he can visualize the notes you play! How cool is that?
